Frequently Asked Questions

To lend your car through our website, you will need to create a listing with details about your car and availability. You can then accept or decline requests from potential borrowers. Once a request is accepted, you and the borrower will need to agree on the terms of the loan (e.g., rental period, pickup and drop-off location, insurance).

To rent a car through our website, you will need to search for available cars in your area and select the one you want to rent. You will then need to provide your personal and payment information to complete the booking. The car owner will confirm the booking and provide details about the pickup and drop-off location.

Insurance may or may not be included in the rental price, depending on the car owner's policies. You can check the details of the car listing to see if insurance is included, or you can ask the car owner directly. In some cases, you may need to purchase additional insurance to cover the rental period.

Cancellation policies may vary depending on the car owner's policies. You can check the details of the car listing or the booking confirmation email to see if cancellations are allowed and what fees may apply.
